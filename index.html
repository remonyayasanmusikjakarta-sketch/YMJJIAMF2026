Welcome

HOME | Legacy in Motion

After navigating through global uncertainty and a long pause, Jakarta International Summer Music Festival returns in 2026 with renewed spirit, vision, and direction.

HOME | Legacy in Motion is not merely the return of a music camp — it is the continuation of a living and growing educational legacy.

The 2026 edition holds special significance. JISMF alumni — who have now completed studies at various world-renowned music institutions — return as faculty members.

For one full week, participants will undergo an intensive artistic experience: living together in a creative community, attending lessons and masterclasses, and performing in concerts every evening. The festival culminates with the Indonesia Open Competition (IOC) and closes with a Gala Concert.

About

Designed for Competition Readiness

JISMF 2026 is designed as an intensive training programme to help young musicians prepare for music competitions at an international level.

Unlike conventional festivals, JISMF presents a focused learning ecosystem where participants develop artistic strategies, mental readiness, and a deep understanding of global competition standards.

Indonesia Open Competition

In Affiliation with HKIMF

Grand Final — Guangzhou, China

Guangdong Science Center · 16–21 August 2026

The Indonesia Open Competition (IOC) is an open competition that upholds transparency and international standards. Winners will have the opportunity to advance to the HKIMF Grand Final in Guangzhou, China.
